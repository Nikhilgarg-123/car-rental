Maruti Suzuki Wagon R

OVERVIEW OF CAR

Maruti Suzuki Wagon R has now progressed to the new Heartec platform that also underpins the Swift, Baleno, Dzire and the Ertiga. The latest generation model offers more space, a 1.2-litre petrol option and more features over its predecessor. The Wagon R is available in LXi, VXi and ZXi variants. Maruti Suzuki Wagon R is now 19mm longer, 145mm wider, 5mm taller and has a wheelbase of 2,435mm, which again is 35mm more. The vehicle gets a new fascia with sweptback headlamps, new bumper with updated fog lamp housing and a new grille design with a single chrome strip in the centre. The rear section now gets vertical tail lamps along with contrasting chrome and black elements. Inside the vehicle gets a dual-tone cabin with multifunction steering wheel and a seven-inch touchscreen infotainment system. The vehicle also gets a restyled instrument cluster. This time around, the vehicle more safety features in form of ABS with EBD, front seat belts reminder, speed alert system and an optional passenger airbag. The hatchback is available in two petrol engine options. The 1.0-litre three-cylinder engine generates 67bhp of power and 90Nm of torque, and the larger 1.2-litre four-cylinder engine generates 82bhp of power and 113Nm of torque. Both engines are available in five-speed manual and AMT gearbox options.
